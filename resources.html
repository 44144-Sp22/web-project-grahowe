Resources

Take a look at these provided resources. Below you will find two that will help you understand amateur radio better.

QRZ.com: This site will lead you to various FAQ forums and will help you connect with other amateur radio operators. Signing up for an account is easy and free!
ARRL.org: This is the official sponsor of amateur radio activities in the United States. Enjoy several benefits, magazine subscriptions, and more when you sign up for a membership.

Below is a solar chart. This tool is helpful in understanding solar conditions and space weather that could affect amateur radio usage. This chart is provided courtesy of N0NBH, and should refresh throughout the day. It is important to look at a solar chart to plan and prepare for ham radio activity during the morning and evening hours.
