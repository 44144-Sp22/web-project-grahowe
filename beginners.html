Picture yourself in the worst situation possible. The power is out, there's no cell phone service, and the internet is out indefinitely. What would you do? Who would you call? Where would you go?

This may never happen, but what if it does? Ask yourself "Would I be prepared?" If you have an amateur radio license then you're ahead of the game. A simple handheld radio will do just fine for emergency communications. But before we delve deeper, let's take an introduction to amateur radio.
